How to cook human flesh: The greatest recipe of all times!


If you're looking for some delicious hams, look no further than your local school. Girls at the ages of 10–15 and boys at the ages of 10–12 have perfectly sized thighs that make excellent meals. Simply add spices and sugar and voila. Calves also make good drumsticks. Breaded, fried, oiled, buttered, and plane taste phenomenal. Males tend to have tough calves that may be chewy, especially to me. However, for softer sweeter, and sometimes juicier meat, look for girls. Athletes tend to have more leg meat thus more food. Soccer players, cheerleaders, gymnasts, runners, etc. all have perfect leg meat.


Potatos and Debra:


      1 Debra (preferably 5ft long and 2ft wide)
      12 potatoes
      250 liters of human hair oil
      2 lemons
      5 cups of flour
      10 eggs
      8 cups of milk
      50 grams of butter

First, skin your Debra and place it in a large container. Run warm water through your Debra and drain. Leave your Debra in a large oven at 350 degrees fan for 1 hour. Add eggs, milk, butter, lemon, and flour into a bowl and mix well. Chop up potatoes into long strips and coat with the mixture. Leave the mixture in the fridge. Take Debra out of the oven and let them cool. Coat your Debra with the mixture. (the best way to do it is to dip your hands into the mixture and massage his body) Pour your oil into a large container and heat. Once the oil starts bubbling, gently put your Debra into the oil. Cook until golden brown. Cut into 6 parts. Fry your potato strip for 1 minute and arrange it on the plate.
